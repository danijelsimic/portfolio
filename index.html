About Me

Hey there! I’m Danijel, a passionate problem solver with a love for technology. At 29, my journey has been anything but conventional. I started in the metal industry, working with CNC machines, where I first discovered the magic of programming. What began as a curiosity soon turned into a full-blown passion.

As I worked with machines, I realized that programming wasn’t just a tool for me; it was a way to create, innovate, and solve problems. I decided to dive deeper into the world of coding and started teaching myself web development. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript became my new tools, and building websites became my creative outlet.

Eventually, I knew I wanted to turn my passion into a profession. I took the leap and enrolled in Codersbay’s Full Stack Developer program, where I immersed myself in the world of backend development with Spring Boot and beyond. I graduated with excellent results, and today, I’m excited to continue learning and growing as a developer, ready to take on new challenges.
